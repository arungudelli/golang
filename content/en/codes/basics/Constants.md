  <h2>Constants</h2>
  
  
  <p>
    Constants are declared like variables, but with the <code>const</code> keyword.
  </p>
  

  
  <p>
    Constants can be character, string, boolean, or numeric values.
  </p>
  

  
  <p>
    Constants cannot be declared using the <code>:=</code> syntax.
  </p>
