  <h2>Zero values</h2>
  
  
  <p>
    Variables declared without an explicit initial value are given their


    <i>zero value</i>.
  </p>
  

  
  <p>
    The zero value is:
  </p>
  

  <ul>
  
    <li><code>0</code> for numeric types,</li>
  
    <li><code>false</code> for the boolean type, and</li>
  
    <li><code>""</code> (the empty string) for strings.</li>
  
  </ul>
